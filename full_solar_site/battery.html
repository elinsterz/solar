Currently there are challenges with these household batteries that make it hard for widespread use.

1. Cost

It is expensive, therefore not many households can afford to purchase their own battery bank. Most houses send and retrieve their excess or deficient energy from the power grid. This is not ideal because the grid does not store energy, it only redistributes it. Also, the power grid runs on fossil fuels.
